Architecture map

Solve real-world problems — how the platform works

A Sweden-first intelligence system that collects public problem signals, applies compliance and editorial gates, clusters them into founder-relevant pain points, scores each opportunity, and publishes them as trusted insights, rankings, and a searchable library.
Design principles
  • Swedish primary, natural English secondary
  • Evidence-led, founder-useful, editorially clear
  • Human review before publication
  • Transparent scoring, confidence, and source method

Why this architecture

It separates raw signals from public output, which makes the system safer, more debuggable, and more credible. The public site only sees reviewed, anonymized, structured pain points — not unfiltered scraped content.

What makes it founder-useful

  • Every cluster becomes a startup-readable pain point
  • Scores are broken down into visible sub-signals
  • Top 10 and library are fed by the same structured data model

What must not be skipped

  • Source rights and robots/terms review
  • PII redaction and takedown workflow
  • Human editorial validation for high-visibility items
