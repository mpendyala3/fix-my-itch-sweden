Architecture map

Solve real-world problems — how the platform works

A Sweden-first intelligence system that collects public problem signals, applies compliance and editorial gates, scores founder-worthy opportunities, and publishes only the Top 10 problems for each category.

Design principles
  • Swedish first, natural English second
  • Evidence-led and editorially clear
  • Human judgment before publication
  • No public all-problems dump

Source inputs

  • Reddit, Flashback, X and public communities
  • SCB, Vinnova, Konsumentverket, Försäkringskassan
  • Trustpilot SE, Reco, ARN, Hallå Konsument

Collection + compliance

  • Source registry and ingestion cadence
  • Raw evidence store with metadata
  • PII redaction, consent flags and takedown hooks

Scoring + editorial layer

  • Cluster similar complaints into founder-readable problems
  • Score severity, frequency, whitespace and trust
  • Human curation narrows the public product to Top 10 per category

Public experience

  • Category-first Top 10 homepage
  • Architecture route for transparency
  • Swedish-primary with natural English toggle
